1. Verses Restaurant

Verses Restaurant offers fine, French-inspired dishes, prepared with the freshest local ingredients. The intimate atmosphere boasts an impressive wine selection, offered from the stately restaurant’s centrepiece, a beautiful glass cellar. Dig into a menu of surprising flavours, each one offering a little bit of poetry to your plate
2. Kyo Bar Japonais

Right in the heart of Place d’Armes, KYO Bar Japonais offers mouth-watering izakaya creations, creative sushi plates and over 25 labels of sake—the biggest selection in Montreal! The friendly staff, yummy Japanese fare and clever décor (think classic Japanese tavern peppered with Montreal’s signature flair) guarantee to keep you coming back!
3. Bevo Bar + Pizzeria

Traditional Italian cooking served up in a fiery atmosphere—BEVO Bar + Pizzeria offers bumping 5 à 7s, wood fired pizzas, late-night bites, a well-stocked bar, a charming staff…the list goes on! Its warm ambiance caters perfectly to a couple's night out while its two expansive floors make it the optimal gathering spot for large groups.
